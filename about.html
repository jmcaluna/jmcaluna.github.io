About Janyl Mae

Janyl Mae Caluna is a skilled professional with a background that combines education and technology. She started as an English teacher and developed a strong interest in language and communication. Despite the challenges of her initial career, she is dedicated to learning and boldly shifted to the IT industry, specifically in Data Analytics.

Using her knowledge in computer programming and data analytics, she immersed herself in various programming languages and data analysis. She became adept at turning complex datasets into useful insights, bridging the gap between language and logic. Using her keen attention to detail and problem-solving skills, she aims to carve a niche in the Data Analytics field. She plans to use her teaching experience and technical skills to uncover patterns and stories hidden in data sets. Excited to contribute to the evolving world of analytics, she is determined to make a significant impact in data-driven decision-making, combining her educational insight with technical expertise.
